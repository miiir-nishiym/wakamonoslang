Ukeru

ウケる

MEANING

Funny

EXAMPLE SENTENCE

「（友達の話を聞いて）それめっちゃ ウケる！」
→(Listening to my friend's story)That's really Ukeru!

ORIGIN

The word ukeru has existed since the Edo period in Japan. It was used as a dressing room word for comedians to mean receiving applause or having a good reputation at a play. From there, it gradually spread to many people. Ukeru, which means interesting, is a word that evolved from there.
