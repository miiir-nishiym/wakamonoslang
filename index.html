HELLO!

こんにちは

Welcome to our website! Here, we explain popular slang used by Japanese youth in simple English. Many of these words are shortened or used in different ways from their original meaning, so they can be hard to translate directly. This site will help you understand these unique words and how people use them every day. We hope you find it helpful!

SCENE SEARCH
You can search for youth slang by scene. Select the scene that interests you and click on it.

You can search by word. Select the word that interests you and press it.
